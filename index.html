About the Mission

Sahayra Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing immediate relief and long-term rehabilitation to those affected by natural disasters. With the recent floods in Punjab, we have launched an emergency relief drive to deliver food, clean water, medical aid, and temporary shelters to the most vulnerable communities.

  • Food Distribution: Nutritious meals for displaced families.
  • Medical Assistance: Essential medicines and health support.
  • Clean Water Access: Preventing waterborne diseases.
  • Temporary Shelters: Safe spaces for families who have lost their homes.

Our Impact & Updates

  • Over 10,000 meals distributed within the first week
  • Medical camps set up in 5 affected districts
  • Clean water tanks installed in rural flood-hit villages

About Us

Sahayra Foundation – "From Compassion to Care" – is a humanitarian organization driven by the belief that every life matters, especially in times of crisis. Our mission is to extend a helping hand to those facing the toughest days of their lives.

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between those who want to help and those in need, Sahayra Foundation has been actively involved in community-driven relief efforts across India. The Punjab Flood Relief is our most urgent call to action.

Our Core Values

  • Humanity First – Every action we take is for the well-being of people.
  • Transparency – We maintain clear records of every donation and relief effort.
  • Collaboration – We work hand-in-hand with volunteers, local authorities, and global supporters.
