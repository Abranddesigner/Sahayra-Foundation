About the Mission

Sahayra Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing immediate relief and long-term rehabilitation to those affected by natural disasters. With the recent floods in Punjab, we have launched an emergency relief drive to deliver food, clean water, medical aid, and temporary shelters to the most vulnerable communities.

  • Food Distribution: Nutritious meals for displaced families.
  • Medical Assistance: Essential medicines and health support.
  • Clean Water Access: Preventing waterborne diseases.
  • Temporary Shelters: Safe spaces for families who have lost their homes.

Our Impact & Updates

  • Over 10,000 meals distributed within the first week
  • Medical camps set up in 5 affected districts
  • Clean water tanks installed in rural flood-hit villages
